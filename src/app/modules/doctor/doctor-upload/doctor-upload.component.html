The look and feel of your prescriptions can be configured. You can specify a custom prescription header and footer. Your scanned signature, if provided, will be used to sign the prescription.

Your scanned signature will be automatically embedded in your prescriptions. If signature is not updated, you can manualy sign the prescriptions.

Your prescription header. If not specified, MEDePAL will create a default prescription header.

Header not specified

Your prescription footer. If not specified, MEDePAL will create a default prescription footer.

Footer not specified

Is it mandatory to configure my prescription?

No, its optional. If you do not customise, default MEDePAL prescription will be generated. MEDePAL prescription is compliant with the Medical Council of India guidelines.

If enabled, generic name of prescribed medicines, in addition to the brand name, will be printed in the prescription. Else only brand names will be printed.
